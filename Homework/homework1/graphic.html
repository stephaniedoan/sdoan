Aspects of Communication Design

Graphic Design

The Communication Design program at CSU Monterey Bay teaches students essential design knowledge that allows students to create communicative graphic art using programs from the Adobe Creative Cloud.

Students work in Adobe InDesign and Adobe Acrobat to create page layout designs for print and screen. Emphasizes use of type, typesetting techniques, legibility, and design composition. Covers scanning, importing files, and mastering digital prepress production.
