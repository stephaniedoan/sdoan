Aspects of Communication Design

Game Design

The Communication Design program at CSU Monterey Bay students design and create games and interactives using analog and digitally-based authoring tools. Includes project management, production, and asset management of images, text, sound, animation, and computer code.

Students learn how to design 3D character models and how to apply industry best-practice and tools to character designing and modeling process.
